THE LIGHT AND NATURE OF A PHOTOGRAPH

Jose Caballero

Works with more than sensibility

Invitartes partners with José Caballero, the soul of Bodafilms, a production company with a lot of light

José Caballero is the soul of Boda&Films, a study with a wide experience in photography. You could find works with more than sensibility, transfering you into the day that were taken, rather than just recreating a landscape or human figure. In short, images that capture the magic of the whole context.



Invitartes strongly recommend José Caballero to perform your pre-wedding and wedding photos and / or other events. The custom work of this photographer combines perfectly with invitartes procedure.



Jose Caballero is inspired by present and past decades, but revised and renewed at the same way, running a current perspective, with a real look that respects the uniqueness of each event to make them simply unique.
